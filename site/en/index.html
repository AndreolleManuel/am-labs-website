Frequently asked questions

Your questions, my answers.

What does AM Labs offer?

Five core services: website development (€1,800-9,000), custom business applications (€5,500-15,000), ERP / e-commerce connectors (€3,500-8,000), SEO and digital audits (€500-1,500), and monthly maintenance (€150-450/month). Detailed service pages are currently available in French.

Do you work with clients outside France?

Yes. AM Labs is based in Montélimar, France, and works remotely with clients everywhere. Tool Innovation, a tool distributor in Panama, got two products delivered fully remotely - including a complete e-commerce platform. Video calls, collaborative tools, staged deliveries: the process is proven.

How does a project run?

Four steps: 1) Free discovery call (30-60 min) to understand your needs, 2) A scoped quote within 24-48h with clear deliverables, pricing and milestones, 3) Staged development with your validation at every milestone, 4) Launch, training, and a 30-90 day guarantee depending on the service.

How much does it cost?

Professional website: €1,800-3,500. Website with a business module: €4,500-9,000. Custom business application: €5,500-15,000. ERP / e-commerce connector: €3,500-8,000. SEO audit: €500-1,500. Monthly maintenance: €150-450/month. 30% deposit on order, never 100% on delivery.

Why you rather than a classic agency?

Three reasons: a single point of contact from discovery to launch (you talk to the person writing the code), 10 years of field experience before code (I understand how a small business actually runs), and transparent pricing with no hidden fees and no vague "on request".
